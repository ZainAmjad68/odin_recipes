Lasagna Recipe

Description

Crispy Pasta Snack is an easy-to-make recipe that you can prepare in just 20 minutes. Made with dried herbs mixture and boiled pasta, this simple snack recipe is best served with cold or hot beverages. If you are a pasta lover, then try this unique pasta recipe.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup pasta fusili
  • 150 gm shredded mozzarella
  • 1 1/2 tbsp tomato ketchup
  • 1/2 teaspoon powdered salt
  • 1 pinch powdered black pepper
  • 1 cup refined oil

Steps

  1. Boil the Pasta.
  2. Combine the Herbs.
  3. Fry the Pasta.
  4. Mix the Herbs and Serve.
  5. Enjoy the Pasta!
